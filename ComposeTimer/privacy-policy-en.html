ComposeTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

1. Introduction

ComposeTimer (the "App") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

The App may collect the following personal information:

  • App Store account information (for purchase history verification)
  • App usage statistics (crash reports, etc.)

2.2 Non-Personal Information

The App may collect the following non-personal information:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage data
  • Advertisement display and click information

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing app features and improving services
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical issues
  • Providing and managing premium features
  • Displaying advertisements (for free version users)

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob for advertisement delivery. AdMob operates according to its own privacy policy. For details, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Apple App Store

We use the Apple App Store for processing in-app purchases. For details, please see Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Protection

Timer data created in the App is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. If you use iCloud sync functionality, data is synchronized through Apple's iCloud service.

6. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app will delete all data stored on your device. For data synchronized to iCloud, you can delete it from iCloud settings.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. If there are significant changes, we will notify you through the app or on this page.
